About Me

Assistant Professor

Prof. Kuljeet Singh is working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, School of Sciences, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Delhi-NCR, 201003, India. He is also a doctoral research scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Information Technology, Central University of Jammu, Jammu and Kashmir, India.

He received a Master's in Computer Application from the University of Jammu, Jammu and Kashmir, India in 2018. He worked as a lecturer and in-charge head of the department at the Department of Computer Science and IT, Kishtwar campus, University of Jammu, for over two years.

He qualified for the National Eligibility Test (NET) four times consecutively in 2019–21, and the Himachal Pradesh State Eligibility Test (HP-SET) in 2019. In past years, he has published/communicated over 35 SCI and Scopus journal research papers, 10 Scopus-indexed conference papers, 3 Book Chapters, 8 Patents, and 1 authored book.
