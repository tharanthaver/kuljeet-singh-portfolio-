About Me

Assistant Professor

Prof. Kuljeet Singh is working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, School of Sciences, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Delhi-NCR, 201003, India. He is also a doctoral research scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Information Technology, Central University of Jammu, Jammu and Kashmir, India.

He received a Master's in Computer Application from the University of Jammu, Jammu and Kashmir, India in 2018. He worked as a lecturer and in-charge head of the department at the Department of Computer Science and IT, Kishtwar campus, University of Jammu (over 2 years).
